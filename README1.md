# FNP Sales Performance Dashboard

An Excel-based sales analytics dashboard for Ferns N Petals (FNP) that consolidates order data across products, occasions, and cities to provide real-time performance insights and drive revenue optimization.

![Dashboard Preview](DashBoard.png)

---

## 📊 Business Problem

FNP needed a centralized view of sales performance to answer critical business questions:

1. **Revenue optimization** – Which occasions and product categories drive the most revenue?
2. **Operational efficiency** – What are peak ordering hours, and how can fulfillment be optimized?
3. **Geographic strategy** – Which cities generate the most orders, and where should expansion focus?
4. **Product performance** – Which products have the highest revenue potential?

Without a unified dashboard, sales teams relied on manual reports that were outdated by the time they reached decision-makers. This dashboard provides real-time visibility into key metrics, enabling data-driven decisions on inventory, marketing, and operations.

---

## 📁 Dataset Overview

The dashboard analyzes **1,000 orders** across multiple product categories, occasions, and cities. Data includes:

- Order details (order date, delivery date, order time)
- Product information (category, SKU, pricing)
- Customer data (city, occasion)
- Financial metrics (revenue, average customer spend)
- Operational metrics (order-to-delivery time)

**Key attributes**: Order ID, Order Date, Delivery Date, Customer City, Product Category, Occasion, Revenue, Quantity

## 🔍 Key Insights

1. **Total revenue of ₹35.2 lakhs from 1,000 orders**, with an average customer spend of ₹3,521—indicating strong order value but room to increase transaction volume.

2. **Order-to-delivery time averages 5.53 days**, which may impact customer satisfaction. Reducing this by even 1 day could improve repeat purchase rates.

3. **Anniversary and Raksha Bandhan drive the highest revenue** (₹6-7 lakhs each), while Valentine's Day underperforms expectations—suggesting untapped marketing potential for this occasion.

4. **Peak ordering hours are between 11 AM and 7 PM**, with the highest volumes at 3 PM and 5 PM. Staffing customer service and fulfillment during these windows is critical.

5. **Top 5 products (Accusantium Set, Aut Box, Officia Pack) generate 60% of total revenue**, highlighting concentration risk. Diversifying product offerings could reduce dependency on a few SKUs.

6. **Bengaluru, Delhi, and Hyderabad are the top 3 cities by order volume** (20-30 orders each). These metros should be prioritized for targeted campaigns and faster delivery options.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

**Reduce delivery times to improve retention**  
Investigate logistics bottlenecks causing the 5.5-day average. Partner with faster couriers or open micro-fulfillment centers in high-volume cities to achieve 3-day delivery.

**Capitalize on high-performing occasions**  
Double down on Anniversary and Raksha Bandhan with pre-order campaigns, exclusive product bundles, and early-bird discounts to maximize these revenue peaks.

**Revitalize Valentine's Day strategy**  
Launch targeted campaigns 2-3 weeks before Valentine's Day. Offer curated gift sets, personalized options, and express delivery to capture this underutilized occasion.

**Optimize staffing for peak hours**  
Increase customer service and warehouse staff from 11 AM to 7 PM. Consider offering "order within 2 hours for same-day processing" during off-peak times to balance load.

**Diversify product portfolio**  
Develop 3-5 new products to reduce reliance on top sellers. Test new categories (e.g., personalized items, subscription boxes) to expand revenue streams.

**Expand in top-performing cities**  
Invest in marketing and infrastructure for Bengaluru, Delhi, and Hyderabad. Consider same-day delivery pilots in these metros to capture more urgent orders.

## 🧠 Dashboard Features

The dashboard provides interactive analysis through:

- **KPI Cards** – Total Orders (1,000), Total Revenue (₹35.2L), Order-to-Delivery Time (5.53 days), Average Customer Spend (₹3,521)
- **Revenue by Occasion** – Bar chart comparing performance across celebrations (Anniversary, Birthday, Diwali, etc.)
- **Revenue by Category** – Analysis of product categories (Cake, Colors, Mugs, Sweets, etc.)
- **Revenue by Hour** – Time-series chart showing hourly order patterns throughout the day
- **Revenue by Month** – Trend line tracking monthly performance and seasonality
- **Top 5 Products by Revenue** – Bar chart highlighting best-selling items
- **Top 10 Cities by Orders** – Geographic distribution of order volume
- **Interactive Filters** – Slicers for Order Date, Delivery Date, and Occasion to drill down into specific segments

All visualizations update dynamically based on filter selections, enabling stakeholders to explore data without technical expertise.
